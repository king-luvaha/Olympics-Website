Sports Played at the Olympic National Games

Click on the button to access location of the sport venue

Swimming

Swimming is an Olympic sport that features various disciplines, including freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Swimmers compete in individual and relay events, with athletes aiming to swim the fastest and break records. The sport demands exceptional technique, endurance, and mental strength, making it one of the most popular and exciting events of the Olympic Games.

KASARANI ACQUATIC STADIUM

Athletics

Athletics is an Olympic sport that includes track and field events such as running, jumping, and throwing. The disciplines range from short sprints and long-distance running to pole vaulting, discus throwing, and more. Athletics requires exceptional physical ability, technique, and mental focus, making it one of the most popular and iconic events of the Olympic Games.

SAFARICOM STADIUM KASARANI

Tennis

Tennis is an Olympic sport that features singles and doubles events for men and women. Players use rackets to hit a ball over a net and aim to win points by landing the ball in their opponent's court. The sport demands exceptional physical ability, hand-eye coordination, and mental strength, making it a thrilling and intense event of the Olympic Games. Tennis.

NAIROBI CLUB TENNIS COURT

Badminton

Badminton is an Olympic sport that requires speed, agility, and precision. Players use rackets to hit a shuttlecock over a net and aim to score points by landing the shuttlecock in their opponent's court. The sport features singles and doubles events. Badminton demands exceptional hand-eye coordination and quick reflexes, making it a dynamic and exciting event to watch.

TRIANGLE SPORTS LTD

Cycling

Cycling is an Olympic sport that requires immense strength, stamina, and skill. It features various disciplines, including road, track, BMX, and mountain biking. Cyclists compete for gold, silver, and bronze medals in individual and team events. The sport demands exceptional physical and mental endurance, making it one of the most challenging and thrilling events of the Olympic Games.

TATU CITY

Decathlon

Decathlon is an Olympic sport that consists of ten track and field events held over two days. Athletes compete in disciplines such as the 100m sprint, long jump, shot put, high jump, and 1500m race. The winner is the athlete with the highest combined score across all events. Decathlon requires exceptional athleticism and versatility, making it a test of the ultimate all-around athlete.
